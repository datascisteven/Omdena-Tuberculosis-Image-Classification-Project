Services

We have developed 4 deep learing models to classify 4 diseases including Covid-19,Breast cancer,Pneumonia and Alzheimer's disease

Covid-19 Detection

This model runs on VGG-16 pre-trained model to classify whether the patient has contracted Covid-19 or not.

Breast Cancer Detection

This model run on Random Forest Classifier to classify whether the patient has developed Breast cancer or not.

Pneumonia Detection

This model runs on pre-trained Sequential, to classify whether the patient has contracted Pneumonia or not.

Alzheimer Segmentation

This model runs on pre-trained Sequential, to classify whether the patient has contracted Alzheimer's disease or not.If the patient has Alzheimer's disease then the model can also detect the type of Alzheimer's disease.
