About

Welcome to DiagnoSys, a deep learning project aimed at detecting and classifying diseases based on MRI images of the brain and X-ray scans of the chest.
We are a team of three dedicated individuals who are passionate about improving healthcare outcomes through the use of cutting-edge technology. We believe that our solution has the potential to revolutionize the way diseases are diagnosed and treated.
By harnessing the power of deep learning algorithms, we are able to analyze medical images with incredible speed and accuracy, helping doctors to make more informed decisions and improving patient outcomes.
Our team includes experts in computer science, data analysis, and healthcare, all working together to create a better future for patients and healthcare providers alike. Thank you for visiting our website. We invite you to learn more about our project and join us on our mission to make a real difference in the world of healthcare.
